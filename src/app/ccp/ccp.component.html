FAQs

Students from all colleges across the globe are welcome to apply.

Yes, multiple students can join Indigo Squad from one college. As a team you would be required to organize events.

As a member of Indigo squad you can enrol in the Mood Indigo Influencer program to upgrade your social media skills and be the digital brand ambassador of Mood Indigo. As an Influencer, you can get direct access to Mood Indigo Influencer meets where you can learn from the best in the industry about content creation and social media management. All you need is an active Instagram account! Stay tuned for more information.

As a part of the program, you start at level one and complete various tasks such as ideating on and executing innovative events, actively planning for social media publicity, strategizing and driving word of mouth publicity campaigns on campus.

There are 4 levels in total. Like in a game, after successful completion of tasks in each level and earning enough points you would get upgraded to the next level.

You will be given a referral code. You can recommend your friends in your college or city to register on CCP. Whenever your friend registers using your referral code, you will earn points.

MI Coins can be earned on successful completion of various tasks that you will be allotted as a member of Indigo Squad. These coins can be used to purchase things such as accommodation, merchandise etc as well as other products from the MI Store. Consider MI coins as the digital currency of Mood Indigo.

You can apply through our website as soon as the selection portal is open. You will be notified after you successfully register. Then you can start contributing and working immediately.
